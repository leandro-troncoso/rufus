RUFUS was a branding project for a gift shop that began operating during the pandemic. The sales method was through social media and home delivery. The brand's strength was its delivery service during a time of restricted movement, while also aiming to build on the concept of "home," which provided a sense of security during that time.



The brand name was defined as RUFUS, derived from "furnarius rufus," the scientific name of the ovenbird. This bird is particularly known for its nest shaped like a house, but it's also a skilled flier. The colors were also chosen based on the bird. For the style and color palette, we aimed for seriousness but not too somber.
